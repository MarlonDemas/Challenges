A letter to my darling

My baby

This is a letter to you my darling to apologise for having acted the way I did. There is no excuse for my behaviour so I want to write you this sincere apology. I want to make it up to you and to do so I have planned an amazing evening ahead for you to show you how much you mean to me. No amount of money in the world is enough for you and that is why I bought you a dozen of your favourite flowers with a box of your favourite chocolates along with a dinner I planned for you with your favourite dish prepared by yours truely.
